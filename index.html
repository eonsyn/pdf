Physics – Motion in a Straight Line

Time: 1 Hour

Q1. What is the displacement of an object moving in a straight line with uniform velocity?

(A) \( s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \)

(B) \( s = vt - \frac{1}{2}at^2 \)

(C) \( s = ut \)

(D) \( s = \frac{v^2 - u^2}{2a} \)

Q2. If an object starts from rest, which formula determines its velocity after time \( t \)?

(A) \( v = u + at \)

(B) \( v = at \)

(C) \( v = \frac{s}{t} \)

(D) \( v = \frac{u + v}{2} \)
